Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • â…“ cup creamy peanut butter, to taste
  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1-2 tablespoon strawberry preserves, to taste
  • kiwi, chopped & peeled for relish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel banana.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on banana to give it a "hot dog-like" appearance.
  3. Place in a hot dog bun.
  4. Drizzle with strawberry preserves (ketchup) and chopped kiwi (relish), if desired.
